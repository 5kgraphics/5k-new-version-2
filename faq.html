Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our digital assets and services.

Digital assets are electronic resources that have value and can be owned, stored, and transferred electronically. In our context, they include UI kits, templates, fonts, presets, brushes, textures, e-books, courses, audio files, and videos that designers and developers can use in their projects.

Yes! All digital assets on our platform are completely free to download. We believe in supporting the creative community by providing high-quality resources at no cost. We monetize through non-intrusive ads and optional donations from supporters who find our resources valuable.

Simply browse our collection, click on any asset you're interested in, and click the "Download Now" button. You may need to wait for a short timer (which helps support our free service) before the download begins. The asset will be downloaded directly to your device.

License terms may vary for each asset. Most of our assets are available for both personal and commercial use, but we recommend checking the specific license included with each download. If you're unsure, you can always contact us for clarification.

The short timer allows us to display advertisements, which helps us keep all our digital assets free for everyone. This minimal wait time enables us to maintain and grow our collection while providing high-quality resources at no cost to you.

You can support us by making a donation through our Support page, sharing our website with others, subscribing to our newsletter, or simply using and enjoying our free resources. Every bit helps us continue providing quality digital assets to the creative community!

No account is required to browse and download assets. We believe in making the process as simple as possible. However, you can subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates about new assets and features.

We regularly update our collection with new digital assets. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when new resources are added. We aim to provide fresh, relevant content for designers and developers on a consistent basis.

File formats vary depending on the asset type. UI kits may include Figma, Sketch, or PSD files. Templates come in various formats like HTML, React, or WordPress. Fonts are typically in OTF, TTF, or WOFF formats. Each asset page shows the available formats and file sizes.

You can reach us through our Contact page or use the live chat widget in the bottom right corner of the site. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please mention it in your message subject line.
